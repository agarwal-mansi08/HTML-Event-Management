Our management team has handled events of every size with clientele ranging from individuals, celebrities, to corporation of all sizes. Each event is planned with attention to every detail; while providing absolute privacy to you & your guests.

We represent many of the newest and best venues in Pune / Mumbai and surrounding cities.

MA & Co. as a Top event management company in Pune also offers consultancy to any Event. Our business philosophy is based on confidence, integrity, flexibility; and attention to detail and personal service.
